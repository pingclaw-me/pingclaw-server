Use PingClaw with ChatGPT by opening the PingClaw GPT. The first time you open it, ChatGPT will ask you to connect your PingClaw account — click Allow and approve the connection.

Once connected, ask ChatGPT anything location-aware — for example "Where am I?", "What's near me?", or "How far am I from the office?". ChatGPT will check your live location from PingClaw and answer.
